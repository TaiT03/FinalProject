Fruits & Vegetables



In this page we will be giving you information about fruits & vegetables such as different ways you can prepare meals & drinks to incorporate them into your diet, how they are good for you, & other ways you can use them besides eating & drinking.



Fruits

First we have fruits, now there are 7 different categories of fruits (Apples/Pears, Berries, Citrus, Melons, Stonefruit, Tomoatoes/Avacadoes, & Tropical/Exotic,) so we are going to give you a break down on exactly what each catgeory is & the types of fruits that are in them.


Apples/Pears


Apples:
Apples are considered to be nutrient packed fruits. They offer Vitamin E which is a fat-soluble antioxidant, Vitamin B1(or Thiamine) which is needed for growth development, & Vitamin B6 which is essential for brain development. Apples have many other great health benefits when you eat them such as lowering your risk for heart disease & diabetes. Apples are known help against certain types of cancers such as lung, breast & digestive tract cancer. They are also good for gut health, which means they give your gut the bacteria it needs to sustain itself. Aside from eating them, you can use apples in many other ways such as ripening green tomatoes. You can do this by placing 5-6 green tomatoes in a bag with an apple or two (make sure you keep a ratio of 5-6 tomatoes per 1-2 apples for effient ripening!!). Another way you can use apples is including them in your workout ! Place an apple inbetween your thighs while you do crunches as this helps strengthen your core.

Pears:
There are almost 100 different types of pears that are grown worldwide. The most popular types are Barlett, Bosc, & D'Anjou. Pears are very beneficial to your health as they have flavanoid antioxidants, which means that they decrease your risk of diseases such as heart disease, type 2 diabetes, breast cancer, & ovarian cancer. They have other compounds in them aswell which are shown to fight against other cancers in the bladder, stomach, or lungs. Pears have many vitamins in them such as Vitamin C which helps keeps cells clean & healthy & helping wounds heal, Vitamin K which helps the body produce blood clots & helps wounds heal. They can be a source of Potassium which is considered to be an electrolyte & Copper which is needed in the body to help form red blood cells. With them being packed with water & fiber, they are considered to be a weight loss friendly food. Pears can also be used for things aside from eating, for example you can make a natural soap or body wash & this will help kep your skin clear.
