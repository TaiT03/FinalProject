Fruits & Vegetables



In this page we will be giving you information about fruits & vegetables such as different ways you can prepare meals & drinks to incorporate them into your diet, how they are good for you, & other ways you can use them besides eating & drinking.



Fruits

First we have fruits, now there are 7 different categories of fruits (Apples/Pears, Berries, Citrus, Melons, Stonefruit, Tomoatoes/Avacadoes, & Tropical/Exotic,) so we are going to give you a break down on exactly what each catgeory is & the types of fruits that are in them.


Apples/Pears


Apples:
Apples are considered to be nutrient packed fruits. They offer vitamin E which is a fat-soluble antioxidant,B1(or Thiamine) which is needed for growth development, & B6 which is essential protein metabolism. Apples have many other great health benefits when you eat them such as lowering your risk for heart disease & diabetes. Apples are known help against certain types of cancers such as lung, breast & digestive tract cancer. They are also good for gut health, which means they give your gut the bacteria it needs to sustain itself. Aside from eating them, you can use apples in many other ways such as ripening green tomatoes. You can do this by placing 5-6 green tomatoes in a bag with an apple or two (make sure you keep a ratio of 5-6 tomatoes per 1-2 apples for effient ripening!!). Another way you can use apples is including them in your workout ! Place an apple inbetween your thighs while you do crunches as this helps strengthen your core.
